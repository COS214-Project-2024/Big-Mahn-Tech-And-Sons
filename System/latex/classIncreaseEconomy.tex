\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A concrete command that simulates an economic boom, leading to increased job creation and a rise in citizen net worth. 

The {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classIncreaseEconomy}{Increase\+Economy}}} class inherits from {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classEventsCommand}{Events\+Command}}} and represents an event where the economy experiences significant growth. This can lead to more industrial and commercial buildings, providing new job opportunities and increasing the wealth of citizens. The economic boom also stimulates various aspects of city life, including real estate, consumption, and public services.

When this command is executed, it will trigger processes that expand the cityâ€™s industrial and commercial sectors, improve citizen wealth, and possibly lead to other positive economic effects. The increased economic activity will create a need for additional resources, infrastructure, and services to support the city\textquotesingle{}s growth.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
This class works in tandem with other city management systems to simulate a holistic economic boom.
