\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages the taxation system and financial distribution within the city. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classTaxManager}{Tax\+Manager}} is responsible for overseeing the city\textquotesingle{}s taxation policies, collecting taxes from citizens and businesses, and allocating the city\textquotesingle{}s budget to various departments. It employs the Visitor design pattern to interact with different building types and resources efficiently.
