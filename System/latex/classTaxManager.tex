\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages taxation policies and visits buildings and resources in the city. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classTaxManager}{Tax\+Manager}} is responsible for managing the taxation system in the city. It collects taxes from citizens and businesses and distributes the city\textquotesingle{}s budget to different departments. The \mbox{\hyperlink{classTaxManager}{Tax\+Manager}} also applies tax policies and interacts with buildings and resources using the Visitor pattern.
