\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Notifies the Utilities Department to address issues or prepare for increased demand. 

This could involve actions like increasing power supply, fixing water supply issues, or improving waste management. The PR Department mediates by sending this notification on behalf of observed buildings or citizens.
