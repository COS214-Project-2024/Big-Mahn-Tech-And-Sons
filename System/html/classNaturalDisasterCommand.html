Detailed Description

Command class for handling the effects of natural disasters on infrastructure.

This class is responsible for managing the impacts of natural disasters, including building damage, route blocking, and infrastructure repairs. It interacts with the Department of Housing and the Department of Transportation to mitigate the effects of disasters on the city's infrastructure and residents.
